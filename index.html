What is Valorant?
Valorant is a free-to-play first-person shooter (FPS) game developed and published by Riot Games, the same company behind League of Legends. It was officially released on June 2, 2020, for Microsoft Windows.
🕹️ Gameplay Overview
Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter, combining precise gunplay like Counter-Strike with unique abilities from agents (similar to Overwatch). The main game mode is plant/defuse, where one team tries to plant a spike (bomb) while the other team defends or tries to defuse it. Matches are played in rounds, and the first team to win 13 rounds wins the match.
